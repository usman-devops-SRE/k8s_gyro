About Us

Gyro Cafe is our very popular mediteranean restaurant. We are
known for our one of a kind Lamb gyro and Chicken Gyro which
can be served in a pita bread sandwich, naan, or over rice with salad
toppings of your choice. Our first location did so well, that we
decided to open another Gyro Cafe location. Gyro Cafe fits right in
with the diversity of Ditmas Park. Plenty of different kinds of food
from different cultures. Gyro Cafe introduces one of the many
food cultures of the middle east to Brooklyn. To spread the taste of
the middle east to Staten Island, we opened Shawafel House in the
beautiful neighborhood of Elm Park. We serve our famous gyro, and
added falafel to the menu which can be served in a sandwich, or over
rice. Check out our Shawafel House Menu.
